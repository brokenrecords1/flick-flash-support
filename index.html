Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan my notes?

Tap the Scan Notes button on the home screen, then choose Camera to take a photo or Photo Library to select an existing photo. FlickFlash will automatically generate flashcards from your notes.

How many free scans do I get?

Free users get 5 scans per month. This resets on the 1st of each month. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited scans.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions → FlickFlash and tap Cancel Subscription.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the app, go to the Account tab, tap Upgrade, and tap Restore Purchases at the bottom of the paywall screen.

Do I need an account to use FlickFlash?

No — an account is completely optional. All core features including Premium work without creating an account. An account allows you to sync your flashcard sets across devices in a future update.

How do I delete my account?

Go to the Account tab, sign in, and scroll to the bottom where you'll find a Delete Account option. This permanently deletes your account and all associated data.

The AI generated incorrect flashcards — what do I do?

You can edit any generated card by long pressing on it in the Flashcards view. This opens an editor where you can fix the question, answer, or card type.
